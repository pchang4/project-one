Muhammad Ali (Cassius Marcellus Clay)

Age

He died on June 3, 2016 at the age of 74.

Hometown

He was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky, and began training as an amateur boxer when he was 12 years old.

What he did

Muhammad Ali born Cassius Marcellus Clay, January 17, 1942 - June 3, 2016) was an American professional boxer, activist, and philanthropist. He is widely regarded as one of the most significant and celebrated sports figures of the 20th century. From early in his career, Ali was known as an inspiring, controversial, and polarizing figure both inside and outside of the ring

Notiable Achievements

  • WBC Heavyweight Champion
  • WBA Heavyweight Champion
  • NABF Heavyweight Champion
